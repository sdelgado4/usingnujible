using Nujible

How-to and FAQ

How to use Nujible.

1. Clear all existing notifications on your Apple Watch and iPhone. Make sure that notifications are turned on in your Settings.

2. Select how often you want to receive a nudge (2, 4, or 8 minutes).

3. Select how long you want to receive nudges. The free version allows you to select a duration up to 20 minutes. With the upgrade, you can select a duration up to 5 hours.

4. Press START. You will begin receiving notifications (nudges) in the increment that you selected. You can stop anytime.

I upgraded the app, but I am not able to see additional time duration settings.

• Under the time duration menu, tap on “Restore” to reset your settings.

• If this does not work, please delete the app and download it again from the App Store. Your purchase will still be valid.

I am not receiving any notifications.

• Make sure that all existing notifications are cleared on your Apple Watch and iPhone.

• Make sure that notifications are turned on for your iPhone and Apple Watch.

• Make sure that notifications are allowed for Nujible in your Settings.

• If there are still no notifications, please delete the app and download it again from the App Store. This usually fixes most notification problems. (If you purchased the upgrade, you will still have access to all the unlocked settings).

I am only receiving notifications on the iPhone, not the Apple Watch.

• Make sure that all existing notifications are cleared on your Apple Watch and iPhone.

• Make sure that notifications are allowed on your Apple Watch.

• Check that your Apple Watch is strapped close to your wrist. For some Apple Watch models, a loose-fitting band may cause errors in wrist detection, leading to a failure to deliver notifications and vibrations.

• Make sure that the iPhone screen is locked. By default, the Apple Watch will not vibrate when a notification is received while the iPhone is open/unlocked.

• Make sure that your Apple Watch is properly connected to your iPhone. Some Apple Watch models may experience issues maintaining a reliable connection to the iPhone. If this is the case, the Apple Watch may fail to receive notifications.

• If there are still no notifications on the Apple Watch, please delete the app and download it again from the App Store. This usually fixes most notification problems. (If you purchased the upgrade, you will still have access to all the unlocked settings).

I cannot start a session from my Apple Watch.

• Force close the app from your Apple Watch and try again.

• Make sure that your Apple Watch is properly connected to your iPhone. Some Apple Watch models may experience issues maintaining a reliable connection to the iPhone. If this is the case, the Apple Watch may fail to run apps properly.
